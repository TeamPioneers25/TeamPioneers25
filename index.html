Manifesto-2025

Our Vision & Promises

1. Enhanced Security for Peace of Mind

  • Increase and train efficient security personnel.
  • Double the number of CCTV cameras for better coverage.
  • Implement effective identification with IR stickers.
  • Clearly mark parking spaces by flat number for easy monitoring.
  • Plan for digitization of security systems in the long run.

2. Smart Parking Management

  • Organize parking with clear markings and monitoring.
  • Ensure hassle-free parking for all residents.

3. Boosting Community Revenue

  • Generate revenue through periodic stalls, digital advertisements, and flea markets.
  • Use funds to improve community facilities.

4. Optimizing Maintenance Costs

  • Stabilize maintenance fees to avoid sudden hikes.
  • Offer attractive discounts: 5% on quarterly and 8% on half-yearly payments.
  • Publish all financial and administrative updates on our community website for full transparency.

5. Affordable Internet & DTH Services

  • Expand options for DTH and broadband with scalable, economical plans.
  • Provide free, secure Wi-Fi zones in common areas.

6. Tackling Pest and Snake Issues

  • Implement regular pest control for mosquitoes, rats, and snakes.
  • Ensure a safe and hygienic environment for all residents.

7. Priority Care for Senior Citizens

  • Form a Medical Task Force (MTF) to assist seniors during emergencies.
  • Promote community support and care.

8. Convenient Ironing Services

  • Provide a dedicated ironing space within the campus at nominal charges.

9. Energy Efficiency & Sustainability

  • Reduce electricity consumption by installing solar and sensor lights in common areas.
  • Enforce strict garbage segregation as per BBMP guidelines.
  • Promote responsible e-waste disposal.

10. Community Website & Communication

  • Launch a user-friendly website for all community updates.
  • Share quarterly newsletters highlighting finances, events, and future plans.
  • Improve response time for resident queries and enhance engagement.

11. Festival Celebrations & Community Spirit

  • Organize cost-effective, joyful festival events with support from brands and coordinators.
  • Foster a vibrant, inclusive community culture.

12. Regular Quality Checks & Future Investments

  • Conduct routine inspections of buildings, pipelines, powerlines, broadband, water quality, and more.
  • Build a corpus fund for future maintenance like exterior painting and equipment upgrades.
